Our Mission

The mission of Metro Line 2 in Bangladesh is to develop a safe, reliable, and modern mass transit system for urban commuters. It aims to reduce traffic congestion and air pollution by promoting the use of public transport over private vehicles. By enhancing connectivity and ensuring inclusive access, it supports sustainable urban development and improved quality of life.
